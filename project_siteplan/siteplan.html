Vacay Planner

Your digital travel companion

Site Name

Vacay Planner — This name represents a digital travel companion designed to help users organize trips, manage bookings, and create unforgettable memories. It captures the idea of vacation and smart planning combined.

Optional domain: vacayplanner.com

Site Purpose

The purpose of Vacay Planner is to simplify trip organization by providing users with tools to plan destinations, track itineraries, and store booking details. The site aims to make travel stress-free and enjoyable for individuals, families, and groups.

Scenarios

  • How can I organize all my trip bookings in one place?
  • Can I save destinations and build an itinerary before booking?

Color Schema

Primary Color: Deep Teal (#0b3d3a) — used for headers, navigation, and highlights.

Secondary Color: Soft Sand (#d7c9a3) — used for backgrounds and accent areas.

Typography

  • Headings: Bona Nova SC — gives a clean, elegant, and travel-inspired look.
  • Body Text: League Spartan — modern, simple, and easy to read for all users.

Wireframe

Mobile View

• Top navigation with logo and hamburger menu
• Hero image with site tagline
• About section overview
• Contact info and social icons stacked vertically

Desktop View

• Header with logo and horizontal navigation
• Large banner image at top
• Three-column section for “Plan”, “Book”, “Explore” highlights
• Footer with social links and copyright
