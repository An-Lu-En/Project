About Vacay Planner

Vacay Planner is your go-to digital travel companion. We help you organize trips, manage bookings, and create unforgettable memories — all in one place. Whether you’re planning a quick weekend getaway or a month-long adventure, our platform makes it simple and stress-free.

Reason for Choosing the Subject

People are becoming more conscious about what they eat and how it affects the planet. This topic allows me to explore design and development through a socially positive theme, while also practicing responsive layout, forms, and JavaScript interactivity.

Color Scheme

#3FA34D – Leaf Green: used for headings and buttons.

#F2E8CF – Natural Beige: background and highlight areas.

Typography

Headings: Bona Nova SC – adds an organic, editorial look.

Body Text: League Spartan – clean and simple for easy reading.

Accent Text: Italics or lighter weight for quotes and tips.

Wireframe Sketches

Mobile: Single-column layout, logo at top, hamburger menu, featured restaurant cards stacked vertically.

Desktop: Two-column grid, horizontal nav, large hero image banner with overlay text.
